# Claudia Yacht

Premium website for **Claudia Yacht**, offering exclusive private yacht charters and unforgettable maritime adventures in the Azores.

## ⛵ About Claudia Yacht

Claudia Yacht specializes in luxury maritime experiences in the Azores. Whether you're seeking intimate private charters, exciting shared yacht tours, or magical sunset experiences, Claudia Yacht delivers premium service and unforgettable moments on the Atlantic.

## 🌊 Experiences Offered

- **Private Yacht Charters** - Exclusive experiences for your group
- **Shared Yacht Tours** - Join other adventurers on curated tours
- **Sunset Cruises** - Romantic and breathtaking evening experiences
- **Island Explorations** - Discover the beauty of the Azores from the sea
- **Overnight Stays** - Extended maritime adventures
- **Marine Encounters** - Experience local wildlife and marine life

## 📍 Location

Based in the Azores, Portugal - a premier maritime destination known for whale watching, stunning coastlines, and pristine waters.
